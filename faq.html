Common Questions About Our Services

Can't find your answer below? Feel free to contact us directly and our team will be happy to help!

Pricing & Packages

What is your pricing structure? +

Our pricing varies based on the service and complexity. Bridal makeup starts from PKR 15,000, party makeup from PKR 3,000, facials from PKR 2,500, and hair services from PKR 1,500. We offer special package discounts for group bookings and bridal parties. Contact us for detailed pricing on specific services.

Do you offer package discounts? +

Yes! We offer attractive discounts on packages. Our Bridal Package includes makeup, hair, and skincare treatments at discounted rates. Group bookings of 4 or more clients also receive special discounts. Contact us to discuss your specific needs and get a customized quote.

Can I book multiple services at once? +

Absolutely! You can book multiple services on the same day. Many of our clients combine services like makeup and hair styling, or facial and skincare treatments. We'll schedule them efficiently to give you the best experience. Let us know your requirements when booking.

Bookings & Appointments

How do I book an appointment? +

You can book an appointment in multiple ways: Fill out the contact form on our Contact page, email us at glamourbeauty@gmail.com, message us on WhatsApp, or call us directly. We'll confirm your booking and discuss any specific requirements or preferences you have.

What is your cancellation policy? +

We request 24 hours notice for cancellations to allow us to accommodate other clients. Cancellations made within 24 hours of your appointment may be subject to a cancellation fee. However, we're flexible and try to work with our clients whenever possible. Contact us for specific circumstances.

How much time should I book for each service? +

Bridal makeup typically takes 1.5-2 hours including trial sessions. Party makeup takes 1-1.5 hours. Hair styling takes 45 minutes to 1.5 hours depending on complexity. Facials take 45 minutes to 1 hour. Skincare treatments vary. We'll advise you on the exact duration when you book.

About Our Services

Do you offer bridal makeup trial sessions? +

Yes! We highly recommend a trial session before your wedding. This allows you to approve the makeup look, test the durability with your dress, and make any adjustments. Trial sessions are included in our Bridal Package or can be booked separately at a nominal fee.

What products do you use? +

We use only premium, international, salon-grade beauty products from trusted brands. All products are dermatologically tested and suitable for all skin types. We use long-lasting, waterproof formulas for makeup and high-quality professional products for skincare and hair treatments.

Are you experienced with all skin tones? +

Yes! Our team is trained and experienced in working with all skin tones and types. We understand the unique needs of different skin tones and use color-matched products to ensure a flawless finish for everyone. We celebrate diversity and ensure every client feels beautiful.

Hygiene & Safety

What are your hygiene standards? +

We maintain the highest hygiene standards. All tools are sterilized using professional sterilization equipment, facilities are sanitized regularly, and we follow strict protocols between each client. We use disposable applicators for makeup and fresh towels for every client. Your health and safety are our top priority.

Do you follow COVID-19 safety protocols? +

Yes, we follow all recommended safety protocols. Our salon is well-ventilated, we provide hand sanitizers, wear masks as needed, and maintain proper distancing. We're committed to providing a safe environment for our clients and staff while delivering excellent service.

Special Requests & Customization

Can I bring makeup references or inspiration photos? +

Absolutely! We love when clients bring reference photos and inspiration ideas. This helps our team understand exactly what you're looking for and ensures we create a look you'll love. The more details you share, the better we can customize the service to your preferences.

Can a friend or family member attend my appointment? +

Yes! We welcome friends and family to attend appointments. Having a support person can be reassuring, especially for bridal appointments. Let us know in advance so we can arrange comfortable seating and ensure everyone feels welcome in our salon.
